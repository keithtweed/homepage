My hobbies include coding, amateur radio, fountain pens, mechanical keyboards, gaming, and tea.

I currently have a Basic with Honours certification in amateur radio, and am working to study towards an Advanced certification in the future. I mainly operate on VHF/UHF analog and DMR, but am hoping to get onto HF in the near future. Amateur radio allows me to learn more about electronic components and radio wave behaviour and propogation at a hobbyist level, and allows for contact with people around the world.

I enjoy writing with fountain pens since it allows for a more unique and fun experience with an everyday task. It can make revision and note-taking much more interesting with a variety of fun colours and pens that all behave differently, and more intricately. Plus, it can help to save plastic waste from disposable pens by having reusable, more durable pens.

In a similar vein, mechanical keyboards can make the typing I do on a daily basis more enjoyable, and can have ergonomic benefits as well. I switch between a 60%, an ortholinear 40%, and a split ergo 40%, all with linear switches. I've got a numberpad in the works as well, and will attempt to lube the switches for that myself to get a smoother feel to the key presses.
